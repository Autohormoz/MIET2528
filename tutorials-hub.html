Problems are available from day one. Solutions and video walkthroughs unlock on a schedule — here's why.

You'll notice the solution doesn't appear right away, and the video takes even longer to show up after that. That's deliberate, not an oversight: solutions unlock once everyone's actually been through that week's tutorial, and the video waits a further week after that. Wrestling with the written solution on your own first is where most of the real learning happens — the video is there afterwards to fill in whatever's still unclear, not to replace that struggle.
